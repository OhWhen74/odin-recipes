Ravioli

Description

Square or round pillow shaped stuffed pastas, similar to dumplings. They have a filling that can consist of cheese, meat, and/or vegetables. Ravioli are formed by having one layer of flat pasta topped with filling and then placing another flat layer on top to form a pillow.

Ingredients

  • Whole-Milk Ricotta
  • Fresh spinach
  • Shredded mozzarella
  • Egg
  • Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • Salt and pepper
  • Homemade pasta dough
  • Marinara sauce

Steps

  1. Create the filling
  2. Divide dough into four pieces
  3. Roll out dough into a long, rectangular shape
  4. Cut out each sqare and seal dough
  5. Add ravioli one by one into boiling salted water
  6. Add marinara sauce
