Tortellini

Description

A ring-shaped Italian pasta stuffed with cheese or meat that is most traditionally served in broth, though other sauces—including those made from tomato, mushroom, or meat—are also popular.

Ingredients

  • Herbs and spices
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Cream cheese
  • Cheese tortellini
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh spinach

Steps

  1. Cook the tortellini
  2. Add the butter to a large skillet
  3. Add the mushrooms
  4. Add the broth
  5. Stir in the heavy cream and parmesan
  6. Season to taste
